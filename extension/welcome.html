Worldglass

See the world in its own words. Here's everything in 30 seconds.

1Select any word

Highlight text in Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hebrew, Jawi, Malay, French, German, or Spanish — on any page. Worldglass detects the language and shows readings, meaning, and examples. Try it right here:

我每天用电脑学习中文。

2Go deeper

Click any character for the words built from it (电 → 电话 · 电脑 · 电影), click words inside definitions to follow meaning inward, and use the 普/粤/闽/潮 and 简/繁 chips in Chinese popups to switch readings and script.

3Read anything — even images

Click the floating 文 button (bottom-right of every page), press Alt+Shift+O, or right-click → Worldglass. Then drag a box over anything: real text is read instantly, and text inside images is recognized on your device.

4Private by design

Dictionaries live on your device and lookups never touch a server. Some large dictionaries download once on first use, then stay local.
